About the Program

The NMIMS Online MBA is a 2-year postgraduate degree program offered under the Centre for Distance and Online Education (CDOE). It is 100% online and assists students in becoming industry-ready professionals with practical learning experience, including guidance from renowned academicians and 24×7 access to live and recorded lectures. Students are also provided with e-books, journals, and lecture transcripts to support self-paced study throughout the program.

The course is offered in 5 industry-centric specializations. NMIMS has 40+ years of excellence in multidisciplinary education, making it an ideal choice for building a successful professional career. The NMIMS online MBA follows a KSA (Knowledge–Skill–Attitude) framework that develops core competencies for workplace success.

Eligibility Criteria

Education Qualification

Bachelor's degree (10+2+3) from a recognised university in any discipline.

Minimum Score

Minimum 50% aggregate at graduation level. 45% for SC/ST/OBC/PwD candidates.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is NMIMS online good for an MBA?
Yes. With world-class academic quality, 120+ IIT/IIM faculty, KSA-framework learning, and strong career services, NMIMS Online is an ideal and good choice for an MBA. It is NAAC A+ accredited and UGC-recognised.
What is the cost of NMIMS online MBA?
The NMIMS Online MBA costs ₹1,96,000 for the entire 2-year course on a one-time payment plan. Alternatively, ₹55,000 per semester (total ₹2,20,000) or ₹1,05,000 per year (total ₹2,10,000). No-cost EMI starts from ₹8,750/month.
What is the average package for NMIMS distance MBA?
The average package for the NMIMS distance MBA varies between ₹4 LPA and ₹7 LPA depending on the role and specialization. Top roles like Financial Analyst can earn up to ₹11 LPA.
Is an NMIMS online degree valid?
Yes. The UGC has recognised the NMIMS online degree program, making it equivalent to a regular degree course. It is accepted by employers across India and internationally.
Which is better: Symbiosis or NMIMS for Online MBA?
Both are globally recognised with valid degree programs. Symbiosis has stronger international rankings while NMIMS offers more specialization options and broader alumni network. Our counsellors can help you compare both for your specific goals.
