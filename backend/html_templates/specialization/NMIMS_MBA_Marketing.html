About the Specialization

The NMIMS Online MBA in Marketing Management is a 2-year postgraduate program that guides students with traditional and new-age marketing strategies. It offers an opportunity to explore contemporary marketing tactics with a regional and global marketing overview.

Working professionals can enrol as it allows candidates to study at their own pace with access to live, online, and recorded lectures. The course ensures international academic standards via guidance from world-class faculty who have graduated from IITs and IIMs.

Students learn to develop integrated marketing campaigns across diverse platforms and markets upon successful completion. The NMIMS Online MBA follows a KSA (Knowledge–Skill–Attitude) framework.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the eligibility criteria for NMIMS Online MBA in Marketing Management?
A valid 3-year bachelor's degree with 50% aggregate marks is the eligibility criterion (45% for reserved category students).
How is the NMIMS Online MBA in Marketing different from traditional MBA programs?
The personalised student support services, top-notch ed-tech facilities, placement assistance, and dedicated career services make the NMIMS Online MBA one step ahead of traditional programs.
What are the key skills I will learn in NMIMS MBA Marketing?
You will learn to craft marketing campaigns, gain expertise in business laws, hands-on experience in marketing analytics, brand management, digital marketing, and integrated marketing communications.
What is the duration of the NMIMS Online MBA in Marketing Management?
The duration is 2 years with a validity of 4 years — meaning if you take a break, you still have time to complete within the validity period.
Does NMIMS MBA Marketing offer industry collaborations or internships?
Yes, the NMIMS Online MBA in Marketing Management offers industry collaborations and internships under its dedicated "Career Services" department.
