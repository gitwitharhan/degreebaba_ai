About NMIMS Online

The Narsee Monjee Institute for Management Studies (NMIMS) was established in 1981. Since then, NMIMS has been among the top institutions in India offering quality education matching global standards. To make higher education more convenient and accessible, NMIMS initiated its Centre for Distance and Online Education (CDOE) in 1994, also known as NMIMS Online CDOE.

NMIMS Online CDOE offers several online undergraduate, postgraduate, and MBA (WX) programs. The courses are completely online and delivered through virtual learning platforms consisting of e-books, journals, and lecture transcripts with 24×7 access.

Choosing NMIMS Online is a strong choice — the university has a vast network of 82,000+ alumni across the world, 40+ years of legacy, 157,000+ lives transformed, and 20,000+ recruiters shaping dream careers for students.

Examination Process

The NMIMS Online examination process is similar for both UG and PG programs. Exams are conducted through an online proctored platform allowing students to appear from anywhere in the world — all they need is a good internet connection, a laptop, and a working webcam.

Online Proctored Exams

Appear from anywhere globally. Requires a laptop, webcam, and stable internet connection. No need to visit any examination centre.

Evaluation: IA + TEE

Assessment follows an Internal Assessment (IA) + Term End Examination (TEE) format, ensuring a comprehensive evaluation of knowledge and skills.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is an NMIMS online degree valid?
Yes, every online degree offered at NMIMS Online is UGC-recognised and fully valid. The degrees are equivalent to on-campus degrees and are recognised by employers across India and globally.
What are the fees for NMIMS online courses?
Fees range from ₹18,000 to ₹55,000 per semester for UG and PG programs, while the executive MBA (WX) is approximately ₹4,00,000 for the complete course. EMI options are available.
Is the NMIMS online MBA good?
Yes, the NMIMS Online MBA is one of the most prominent online MBA courses in India. The program is NAAC A+ accredited, taught by IIM/IIT faculty, and has strong placement support with 500+ hiring partners.
Who is eligible for the NMIMS online MBA?
Any candidate with a valid 3-year bachelor's degree from a recognised university with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks is eligible for the NMIMS Online MBA.
Which is better, Manipal or NMIMS for online MBA?
Both universities are UGC-recognised with slightly different specialization options. NMIMS is stronger in finance and management while Manipal offers broader tech-focused specializations. Our counsellors can help you choose the right one based on your goals.
